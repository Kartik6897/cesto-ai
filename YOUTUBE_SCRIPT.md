# 🎬 YouTube Video Script & Storyboard: CESTO AI

This document outlines a professional **5-minute video production script** for presenting CESTO AI on YouTube. It includes visual cues, on-screen text, background music moods, and a complete narration script.

---

## 📹 Video Metadata
*   **Title**: Stop Wasting Money on LLMs! Meet CESTO AI: The Autonomous Cost Controller 🚀
*   **Description**: 
    Are your company's AI API bills spinning out of control? Discover CESTO AI (Cost Estimator and Controller for AI), an open-source autonomous agent system designed to track token usage, enforce budget policies, cache prompts, and automatically route queries to cheaper models. 
    
    In this video, we run a live demo of the CESTO AI proxy, showcase its cyber-emerald dashboard, and explore the future of autonomous agent-led cost optimization.
    
    Clone the code here: https://github.com/Kartik6897/cesto-ai
*   **Target Length**: 5 Minutes (approx. 750 spoken words)
*   **Background Music**: Futuristic, upbeat tech-synth beat (low volume under narration).

---

## ⏱️ Timeline & Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Hook (0:00 – 0:45)
*   **Visuals**: 
    *   *0:00 - 0:15*: Fast-paced montage of news headlines about "Skyrocketing AI Costs," "API bill surprises," and a mock credit card charge of $15,000 flashing red.
    *   *0:15 - 0:45*: A talking head or dynamic kinetic typography showing the text: "AI is amazing. But it is a financial black box." Transition to a clean screenshot of the CESTO AI dashboard glowing green.
*   **Audio/Voiceover**:
    > "Every company is building with AI. We are spinning up agents, chatbot assistants, and automated data pipelines. But at the end of the month, a surprise arrives: a massive, out-of-control API bill from OpenAI or Anthropic.
    > 
    > How do you know which department is spending what? How do you prevent recursive loops from draining your budget overnight?
    > 
    > Meet **CESTO AI**—the autonomous Cost Controller and Estimator built for business agents. It acts as an intelligent proxy layer that intercepts, tracks, and actively optimizes your AI expenses."

---

### Scene 2: How It Works – The Proxy Architecture (0:45 – 1:45)
*   **Visuals**:
    *   *0:45 - 1:15*: A clean 2D animation showing the Mermaid workflow: Client App ➡️ CESTO Proxy ➡️ AI Provider. Highlights the proxy intercepting the request.
    *   *1:15 - 1:45*: Zoom into the proxy layer. Text badges flash: `Rate Limiting`, `Budget Check`, `Model Fallback`, `Prompt Caching`. The diagram shows an expensive query being rerouted to a mini model.
*   **Audio/Voiceover**:
    > "CESTO AI sits silently between your applications and your LLM providers. Instead of connecting your code directly to OpenAI or Gemini, you route requests through CESTO. 
    > 
    > Here is what happens in milliseconds: First, CESTO checks the department's request rates against active limits. Second, it checks the monthly budget. If the Engineering department has breached its limit, CESTO automatically downgrades the model—routing a costly GPT-4 query to a highly efficient GPT-4o-mini instead.
    > 
    > Finally, CESTO applies intelligent prompt caching, checking if the prompt context is repetitive, and instantly applying a 35% discount on input tokens. All metrics are saved to a central telemetry database."

---

### Scene 3: Live Walkthrough & Dashboard Tour (1:45 – 3:30)
*   **Visuals**:
    *   *1:45 - 2:15*: Screen recording of the **CESTO AI Dashboard**. Mouse hovers over the KPI stats (Total Spend, Total Savings, Total Ingested Tokens). Show the beautiful Chart.js charts animating.
    *   *2:15 - 2:45*: Navigate to the **Ingest Center**. Click the **Traffic Simulator** switch. Show the scrolling terminal console lighting up with neon log entries in real-time as mock requests stream in.
    *   *2:45 - 3:30*: Navigate to the **CESTO Agent** tab. Click the chip: *"List active optimizations"*. Show the chatbot responding. Click **Apply Optimization** on the "Downgrade Code Parsing Model" card and show the *Total Savings* counter instantly go up.
*   **Audio/Voiceover**:
    > "Let’s look at it in action. This is the CESTO AI dashboard. You get immediate visibility into your global expenditures, active token counts, and, most importantly, the dollars you've saved.
    > 
    > In the Ingest Center, we can toggle our traffic simulator. Watch as logs stream in from various departments. 
    > 
    > If we head over to the CESTO Agent panel, we see our autonomous costing chatbot. CESTO scans our database logs to flag cost-saving opportunities. For example, it noticed Engineering was running regex string parses on GPT-4. By clicking 'Apply Optimization', CESTO instantly changes the routing policy in the proxy, saving us $840 a month. And we did it in one click!"

## 💡 Production Tips for the Creator
*   **High Contrast Zoom-ins**: Zoom in closely on the scrolling console log and the dynamic Chart.js animations to emphasize the real-time functionality of the app.
*   **Voice Pace**: Speak with an energetic, authoritative tone. CESTO AI solves a high-cost business problem, so emphasizing "savings" and "control" is key.
*   **Mermaid Visualization**: For Scene 2, overlay the Mermaid diagram from the `README.md` directly onto the video using motion graphics.
